Afro-Iranians

Mahdi Ehsaei

Mahdi Ehsaei curated a photographic series of a side of Iran, that is unknown to even Iranians. Ehsaei’s Afro Iranian project captures unique people and landscapes. Iranians ‘who continue the African heritage with their clothing style, their music, their dance, and their oral traditions and rituals’. He published a photobook on the Afro-Iranian community called ‘Afro Iran’ sold on Amazon.
